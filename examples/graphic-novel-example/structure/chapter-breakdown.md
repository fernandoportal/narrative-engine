# Chapter Breakdown

## Chapter 1 - "Receipt"
Isa delivers a sealed memory capsule and recognizes her sister's laugh in the playback bleed.

## Chapter 2 - "Auction"
She infiltrates an elite memory exchange where identity fragments are sold to collectors.

## Chapter 3 - "Echo Tax"
A public records clerk reveals that the sister's legal identity was divided into corporate assets.

## Chapter 4 - "Reassembly"
Isa decides whether to restore her sister's identity or sell it to erase their shared debt.
