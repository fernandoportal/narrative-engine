# Iago Rell (Antagonist)

## Role

Port director protecting his political position and past budget decisions.

## Goal

Prevent public discovery that lighthouse decommissioning created the present
crisis.

## Strategy

Delay authorizations, redirect blame to weather anomalies, and frame Mara as
unstable.

## Pressure Function

He converts institutional power into time loss, forcing Mara into riskier
decisions.

## Thematic Contrast

Iago represents image-first leadership; Mara must choose truth-first leadership.
