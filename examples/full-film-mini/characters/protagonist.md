# Mara Velez (Protagonist)

## Role

Suspended harbor safety inspector leading emergency reactivation of lighthouse infrastructure.

## Wound

She was punished for telling the truth about safety violations, and now doubts whether integrity has practical value.

## Want

Get the hospital ship safely into harbor and prove she was right about infrastructure risk.

## Need

Accept that leadership means sharing risk publicly, not carrying responsibility in silence.

## Arc

From isolated technical expert to visible civic leader who chooses transparency under pressure.

## Scene Behavior Notes

- Speaks in precise operational language when stressed.
- Avoids emotional disclosure until midpoint failure.
- Final act choice is public, not procedural.
