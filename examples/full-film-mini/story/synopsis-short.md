Mara Velez, once the harbor's top safety inspector, has been suspended after exposing maintenance fraud tied to the port authority. On the same night a hospital ship carrying critical patients arrives offshore, a toxic fog surge disables modern navigation and closes every commercial channel.

The only viable path is an obsolete lighthouse grid decommissioned years ago to cut costs. Mara must convince a skeptical skeleton crew to help her relight the chain while Port Director Iago Rell blocks access to avoid revealing his negligence. As conditions worsen, Mara chooses public accountability over self-protection, broadcasts the true risk citywide, and manually restores the final beacon minutes before sunrise. The ship clears the corridor, and Mara's suspension becomes evidence in a larger corruption case.
