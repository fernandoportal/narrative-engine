# Beat Sheet

- **Opening Image:** Dark harbor. Dead beacons. Sirens swallowed by fog.
- **Catalyst:** Hospital ship reports total navigation failure outside toxic corridor.
- **Debate:** Mara is barred from command; crew resists illegal system access.
- **Break into Act II:** Mara overrides lockouts and forms an ad hoc beacon crew.
- **Midpoint:** Three lighthouses come online, but the primary relay tower shorts out.
- **Bad Guys Close In:** Iago orders security to seize control station and halt unauthorized operation.
- **All Is Lost:** Final beacon remains offline; sunrise window drops under 20 minutes.
- **Break into Act III:** Mara streams proof of corruption and rallies volunteers to restore manual power.
- **Finale:** Last beacon ignites; hospital ship enters safely; public inquiry begins.
- **Final Image:** Harbor at dawn, all beacons lit, investigation banners on port gates.
