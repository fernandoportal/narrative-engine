# Beat Sheet (Condensed)

- **Opening Image:** Silent rail yard buried in dust.
- **Catalyst:** Storm trajectory update makes highway evacuation impossible.
- **Debate:** Elena refuses involvement; townspeople reject her help.
- **Break into Act II:** She commits to restarting the rail system.
- **Midpoint:** A partial test run succeeds, but sabotage cuts central power.
- **All Is Lost:** Junction control tower fails with storm less than two hours
  away.
- **Finale:** Elena and crew reroute manually, evacuating most residents.
- **Final Image:** First sunlight over tracks now crowded with survivors.
