# Episode 01: Intake

## Logline

A high-profile juvenile case pushes Mara to challenge the city's predictive sentencing engine in open court.

## A-Story

Mara proves the model penalizes neighborhood history as proxy criminal intent.

## B-Story

Her relationship with her brother fractures when she links his firm to the model vendor.

## Ending Beat

Mara wins temporary release for her client, but receives footage of another "random" arrest generated by the same algorithm.
