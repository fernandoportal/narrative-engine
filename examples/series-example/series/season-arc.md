# Season Arc

## Season Question

Can justice survive when every institution is optimized for prediction instead
of truth?

## Arc Summary

Mara Ibarra uncovers falsified risk models used to target dissident communities.
Each legal victory exposes deeper collusion between courts, insurers, and data
brokers tied to her own family network. By season end, she leaks evidence that
triggers national unrest, but the final reveal shows the system anticipated the
leak and adapted.
