# Season Arc Starter

## Season Premise
A team of former insiders exposes a city-scale deception while confronting the cost of public truth.

## Season Question
What do people owe each other when the system rewards silence?

## Structural Spine
- Episodes 1-2: uncover pattern
- Episodes 3-4: consequences and internal fracture
- Episode 5: irreversible leak decision
- Episode 6: public fallout and redefined mission
