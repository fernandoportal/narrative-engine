# Antagonist Guide

The antagonist is not only a villain; it is the force that challenges the
protagonist's worldview.

## Design Questions

- What does the antagonist want?
- Why is that goal rational from their perspective?
- How does their strategy expose the protagonist's weakness?
- What emotional truth do they embody or distort?

## Practical Rule

Track antagonist pressure at regular intervals so tension does not disappear
between major turns.
