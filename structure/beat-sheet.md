# Beat Sheet Starter

## Opening Image

Show protagonist's current survival strategy in one visual moment.

## Catalyst

Introduce the event that makes inaction costly.

## Break into Act II

Identify the decision that closes the old life path.

## Midpoint

Insert a reframe that changes both stakes and behavior.

## All Is Lost

Demonstrate collapse of old strategy with real cost.

## Finale

Resolve the central conflict through transformed character agency.

## Final Image

Contrast the opening image to prove change.
