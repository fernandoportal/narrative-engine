# Three Act Structure

## Act I - Setup
Purpose: establish ordinary world, flaw, stakes, and inciting disruption.

Deliverables:
- clear protagonist objective by end of Act I
- visible antagonistic force
- irreversible decision launching Act II

## Act II - Confrontation
Purpose: escalate difficulty, expose flawed strategy, and force adaptation.

Deliverables:
- progressive failures and partial wins
- midpoint shift that reframes understanding
- pressure from both external and internal conflict

## Act III - Resolution
Purpose: transform insight into decisive action and thematic closure.

Deliverables:
- all-is-lost consequence acknowledged
- climax resolved by character agency
- final image contrasting opening state
