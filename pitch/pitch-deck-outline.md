# Pitch Deck Outline

1. Title + Tagline
2. Logline
3. World and Tone
4. Core Characters
5. Story Engine (acts / season shape)
6. Visual Language and References
7. Audience and Positioning
8. Production Scope or Format Plan
9. Team / Creator Statement
10. Ask (financing, partners, distribution, collaborators)

Use one idea per slide and keep each page visually focused.
