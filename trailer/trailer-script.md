# Trailer Script Starter

## Hook
A city-wide blackout. Emergency lights reveal thousands waiting in silence.

## Escalation
Fast cuts: legal hearings, data vaults, rooftop confrontations, fractured alliances.

## Signature Lines
- "Truth is not missing. It's paywalled."
- "You built a perfect system for the wrong people."
- "If we leak this, we do not go back."

## Final Push
Clock countdown over crowd noise, then title card and a final unresolved image.
