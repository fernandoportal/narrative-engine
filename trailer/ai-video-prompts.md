# AI Video Prompts

Use these prompts as starting points for teaser/trailer visualization tests.

## Prompt Pattern
[Shot type] of [subject/action] in [location], [lighting], [camera movement], [mood], cinematic realism, high detail.

## Examples
1. Wide shot of a flooded civic plaza at dawn, emergency strobes reflecting on water, slow dolly forward, anxious atmosphere, cinematic realism.
2. Close-up of a cracked ID badge in a gloved hand, sodium-vapor lighting, handheld micro-shake, tense investigative mood.
3. Aerial shot over a train corridor cutting through desert dust storm, hard side light, fast push-in, urgent survival tone.

## Continuity Rule
Lock recurring visual anchors (palette, costume silhouettes, symbols) across prompt batches.
