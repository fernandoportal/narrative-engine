# Thematic Engine

Use this file to articulate what your story means, not only what happens.

## Core Question
What belief is under stress?

## Candidate Thesis Example
People who trade control for safety eventually lose both.

## Theme Delivery Points
- Character choices under pressure
- Repeated image systems (doors, mirrors, weather, machinery)
- Dialogue contrasts between cynicism and hope
- Ending action that proves or challenges the thesis

Theme should be visible in plot mechanics, not isolated in speeches.
