# Emotional Logic

Track why character decisions are emotionally believable.

## Core Questions
- What fear drives the protagonist's default behavior?
- What belief must be dismantled for transformation?
- What does the antagonist represent emotionally?
- What event forces the protagonist to choose change over defense?

## Application
For every major turn in `structure/beat-sheet.md`, note the emotional trigger and resulting action. If a turn feels forced, revise motivation here before rewriting scenes.
