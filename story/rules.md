# Narrative Rules

Define non-negotiable constraints so rewrites remain coherent.

## Rule Types

- **World rules:** what is possible or impossible
- **Tone rules:** acceptable tonal range and limits
- **Character rules:** behaviors characters will not break without explicit arc
  logic
- **Causality rules:** outcomes must emerge from choices and pressure, not
  convenience

## Example Constraints

- No omniscient exposition dumps.
- Antagonistic force appears before midpoint.
- Major twists require prior setup.
