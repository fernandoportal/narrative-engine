# Poster Prompt Starters

## Prompt 1 - Character-Driven

A tense cinematic poster of a determined protagonist standing before a city data wall at night, rain-soaked asphalt reflections, high contrast lighting, minimalist title space, dramatic thriller tone.

## Prompt 2 - System vs Human

A split-composition poster showing a sterile control room above and crowded street protest below, cool vs warm color contrast, symbolic broken line connecting both worlds.

## Prompt 3 - Symbolic Object

Close-up poster concept of a cracked transit map glowing under emergency red light, subtle silhouette in background haze, premium festival-drama aesthetic.

## Usage Note

Keep one consistent title treatment and one recurring symbol across all poster iterations.
